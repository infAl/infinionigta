To install this mod, you need to have Simple Mod Loader installed first.

You can download Simple Mod Loader from github:
https://github.com/infal/modloader/releases


This mod also requires the Infinion Corporation mod
https://github.com/infal/infinion/releases


Once you have Simple Mod Loader and Infinion Corporation installed, extract the contents of this zip file to the avorion/data/scripts/mods/ folder.


Now when you find an Ininion Corporation Outpost it will have a dialog option to buy dangerous goods permits, pirates may drop special Cargo Shielding Modules and you will be notified when Military ships are scanning your cargo
